This recipe uses a combination of dry ingredients, including all-purpose flour, cornstarch, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. The wet ingredients consist of softened unsalted butter, granulated sugar, vegetable or canola oil, large eggs, vanilla extract, distilled white vinegar, red food coloring gel, and buttermilk.
