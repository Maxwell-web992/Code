Swimming

Swimming is an individual or team racing sport that requires using one's entire body to move through water. The main objective in competitive swimming is to swim a specified distance as fast as possible, using specific techniques called strokes.

Cycling

Cycling is a popular recreational activity and competitive sport that involves riding bicycles. It can be done on various terrains, including roads, mountains, and trails. Cycling is not only a fun way to explore the outdoors but also an excellent form of exercise that promotes cardiovascular health and builds lower body strength.

Football

Football, also known as soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is the most popular sport in the world, played by over 250 million players in over 200 countries. The objective of the game is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal.
